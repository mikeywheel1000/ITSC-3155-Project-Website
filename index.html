Our Mission Statement

As Team 18, we strive to work together and assist each other through challenges and obstacles along the process of project creation. We plan to push each other so that way we can meet the timeframe of our end goal. The goal of our group is to easily meet the deadline for every sprint we have along the way, and in the end to create a well working Software Engineering design of an Online Restaurant Ordering System (OROS). We plan to meet all the functionality requirements for the restaurant as well as meeting customer needs. As a team, our biggest strength that will help us in the system creation process is collaboration.

Team Rules:

1.Commit to achieving the goals of the scrum team
2.Commit to each doing a fair share of the work
3.Commit to working on one assignment at a time to avoid falling behind
4.Courage to do the right thing
5.Courage to work through and solve problems
6.Focus on working on each individual sprint at a time
7.Focus on the goal of the team as a whole
8.Be open to any problems or questions within the team
9.Be open if any conflict or obstacle will keep a team member from finishing a portion of the current sprint or project
10.Be open to the idea of changes or new ideas
11.Be respectful to others and their work
12.Be respectful to other time commitments that other group members may have
13.Be respectful to other members ideas
14.Team will meet/communicate online Thursday at 2 P.M. or when needed
15.Team will handle falling behind schedule by meeting and working together to get back on track and on the right time schedule
16.If dealing with an uncooperative teammate, we will first try and talk through it with the teammate, and if change does not occur then we will talk to the professor
